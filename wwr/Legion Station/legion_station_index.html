What is Star Wars Legion?

Star Wars Legion is a table-top miniature war game which pits different armies accross the star wars universe in battles against one another. This website has been designed to help new and veteran players and everyone in between learn more about Star Wars Legion. The following page will give short descriptions about the 4 factions found in Star Wars legion.

The Sinister Galactic Empire focuses on the use of fear to keep the local systems in line. Sporting an army of Troopers clad in protective armor, The Empire is willing to give their all to preserve The Empire. This army is one to be reckoned with. With heroes such as Darth Vader or Boba Fett leading the charge commanders will find the Dark Side is Strong with the Galactic Empire.

Rebellions are built on hope. The Rebel Alliance uses a variety of hit and run tactics to defeat their enemies. Rebel commanders will also find that the force is strong with this army. Units of Wookies trudge fiercly accross battlefields along heroes such as Luke Skywalker and Han Solo. All are welcome to join the cause of creating a free galaxy.
The Seperatist Alliance ( or commonly known as the Confederacy of Independent Systems as well ) is an army that is endless. Tides of Battle Droids march fearlessly into battle obeying the commands of their Commanders without hesitation. Ruthless sith such as Count Dooku lead the charge with a great host of tactical droids. Equality is the aim of the Seperatist Alliance and they will do so at whatever cost.

The Republic has been a beacon of justice and good in the galaxy for over 1000 years. It has kept peace and unity throughout the galaxy until now. With the Crisis of war upon it, The Galactic Republic relies on its Clone army to protect it from falling apart. Lead by fearless Jedi such as Obiwan-Kenobi, the Galactic Republic has the greatest soldiers in the galaxy. Commanders lead their elite forces into battle for the Republic.
